üü° SECTION 2 ‚Äî INTERMEDIATE EXCEL

üß© Intermediate Formulas

  • Text functions ‚Äî LEFT, RIGHT, MID
  • Logical ‚Äî IF
  • Lookup ‚Äî VLOOKUP, XLOOKUP
Text examples
=LEFT("Revenue",4) ‚Üí "Reve"
=RIGHT("Revenue",3) ‚Üí "nue"
=MID("Dataset",3,4) ‚Üí "tase"
Logical example
With A2 = 120: =IF(A2>100,"Pass","Fail") ‚Üí "Pass"
Lookup examples (small table)
Table A2:B4 ‚Äî Apple:10, Banana:20, Cherry:30
=VLOOKUP("Banana",A2:B4,2,FALSE) ‚Üí 20
=XLOOKUP("Cherry",A2:A4,B2:B4) ‚Üí 30
=INDEX(B2:B4, MATCH("Apple",A2:A4,0)) ‚Üí 10
